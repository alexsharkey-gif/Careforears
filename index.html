About Microsuction

Microsuction is the gold standard method for ear wax removal and is widely used by private clinics and the NHS. It is a gentle, precise technique that uses low-pressure suction under direct visual guidance to safely remove excess ear wax.

Our service is suitable for adults of all ages and is ideal for anyone experiencing blocked ears, reduced hearing, discomfort or tinnitus caused by excess ear wax.
